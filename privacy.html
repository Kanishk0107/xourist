Privacy & Booking Policies

1. Booking & Payment Policy

  • 30% advance payment is required to confirm the booking.
  • Remaining amount must be paid 15 days before departure.
  • Bookings without advance payment will not be confirmed.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • 30 days before departure – 20% cancellation charges.
  • 15–29 days before departure – 50% cancellation charges.
  • Less than 15 days – No refund.
  • No refund for no-show or early departure.

3. Refund Policy

  • Refund will be processed within 7–14 working days.
  • Visa fees, permits, helicopter tickets, and government charges are non-refundable.

  • Xourist is not responsible for denied entry due to incomplete documents.

5. Health & Fitness

  • Xourist is not responsible for health issues during the journey.

6. Itinerary Changes

  • No refund will be provided for such changes.

7. Liability

  • Xourist acts only as a tour organizer.
  • We are not responsible for natural calamities, accidents, delays, political issues, or any force majeure situations.

8. Accommodation & Transport

  • Hotels are subject to availability. Similar category hotels may be provided if required.
  • Transportation timing must be strictly followed by all passengers.

9. Code of Conduct

  • All travelers must maintain discipline and respect local customs, temple rules, and fellow passengers.
